What information should I get to resolve the problem?

Obviously, the most important thing to find out is the type of error (trap or IPE) and the exact message given by the system. On top of that, having the following will further help in problem determination:

  • When does the error occur? At boot up? Within an application?
  • Can the customer get out of it? If so, how?
  • What is the hardware configuration of the system?
      Model?
      Adapters? Which slots?
      Memory? (This could be key to a trap 2 and/or D error)
      OEM cards?

      Additional applications and version?
