hypergraphics

Under the Information Presentation Facility, a portion of a bitmap displayed in a help panel or online document, which may be selected by the end user. Selecting such an item causes an event to occur, such as the display of another help panel, a popup window, the dispatch of a message to the parent application or the start of a new application. See also hypertext.
