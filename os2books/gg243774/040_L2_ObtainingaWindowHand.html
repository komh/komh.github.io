Obtaining a Window Handle Using the Switch Entry

hSwitch = WinQuerySwitchHandle(hWnd,0);
ulSuccess = WinQuerySwitchEntry(hSwitch,
                                SwitchData);
hTarget = WinWindowFromID(SwitchData.hwnd,
                          FID_CLIENT);
