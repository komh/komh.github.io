Checking the WIN-OS/2 Initialization File

The PC/3270 Windows installation should have updated the WIN.INI file. Check the C:\OS2\MDOS\WINOS2\WIN.INI file for the following: 

 .
 .
 .
[PCS3270]
DIR=C:\PC3270W\
 .
 .
 .
