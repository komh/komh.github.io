Indicates the cryptographic algorithm used by the local host for encryption (the ESP protocol).

Possible values are:

    CDMF

      40-bit CDMF
    DES_CBC
      Same as DES_CBC_8
    DES_CBC_4
      56-bit DES with four-byte IV
    DES_CBC_8
      56-bit DES with eight-byte IV
