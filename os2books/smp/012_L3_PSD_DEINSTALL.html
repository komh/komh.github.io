PSD_DEINSTALL

PSD_DEINSTALL keywords

    Required, Can Block

Description
    DeInstall the PSD.

    This function is called to release any resources that may have been allocated by the PSD_INSTALL function. A PSD is never de-installed after its Init routine is called.

Mode
    Called in Init Mode; may be called in Kernel Mode.

Entry
    None.

Exit
NO_ERROR
    if the PSD DeInstalled succesfully.
-1
    if the PSD didn't DeInstall.
Notes
    This function may be called after OS/2 is finished with initialization by the Dos32TestPSD API; therefore, the PSD developer must be careful not to use any init mode only PSDHelp's in this function.
