    Implementation

    This API is translated to ImShowIMEDlg(hIMI,ulDlgType,pRegWord) of OS/2 IM. The hIMI of OS/2 is queried from HWND of WIN95. The dwMode of Win95 is translated to ulDlgType of OS/2.
