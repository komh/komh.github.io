    Functional Difference from WIN95

    Undocumented virtual-key codes are not suporrted.

    Due to the OS/2 design, key shift status for inboard keys, such as 'A', '1', etc. cannot be supported. Only pure shift keys such as 'Alt', 'Ctrl', 'Shift' etc. are supported.
