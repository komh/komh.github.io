How to make your Application DBCS-Enabled?

In the previous chapter, you have learned the basic knowledge of DBCS on IBM OS/2 systems.

The best way to understand how to DBCS-enable your application programs is to analyze and copy sample DBCS-capable application programs and do some experimental runs by yourself. In this chapter, we will show you brief but typical DBCS-capable application programs so that you can quickly learn the coding techniques for DBCS-enabling your programs.
