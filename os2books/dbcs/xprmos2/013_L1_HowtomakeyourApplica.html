How to make your Application Internationalized?

In the previous chapters, you have learned the basic knowledge of XPG4 Internationalization and IBM OS/2 systems.

The best way to understand how to internationalize your application programs is to analyze and copy sample application programs and do some experimental runs by yourself. In this chapter, we will show you brief but typical internationalized PM application programs so that you can quickly learn the basic coding for internationalization.
