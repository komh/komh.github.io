Naming Conventions

The following identifiers allow DBCS and can be a mixed string:

  • Column-name

  • Correlation-name

  • Capitalize cursor-name

  • Capitalize host-variable(**)

  • Capitalize index-name

  • Capitalize plan-name

  • Capitalize statement-name

  • Capitalize table-name

  • View-name

The following identifiers do not allow DBCS and cannot be a mixed string:

  • Database-name

  • Authorization-ID

SBCS characters in mixed string identifiers should follow the naming conventions of SBCS DB2/2. DBCS characters can be any valid characters. Monocasing is not applied to DBCS characters.
