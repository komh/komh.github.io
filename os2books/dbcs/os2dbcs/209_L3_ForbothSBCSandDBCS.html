For both SBCS and DBCS

If you provide different editions of your program in order to support different environments such as SBCS, DBCS and different code pages, duplicate effort will be required not only in development but also in maintenance, and distribution cost of NLS support will be increased. To save the work load, an application program is recommended to provide single module with multiple resources.
