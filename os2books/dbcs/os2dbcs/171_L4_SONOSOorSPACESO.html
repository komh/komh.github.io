SO, NOSO or SPACESO

    NOSO

      SO/SI are removed and do not take positions on the target, i.e. EBCDIC data is translated to ASCII in the normal way.
    SO
      SO and SI are translated to 0EH and 0FH respectively.
    SPACESO
      SO and SI are translated to SBCS spaces (20H).

    Either of the last two could be of use for column-sensitive data to avoid column distortion, which the default option NOSO causes because of the SO/SI deletion.
