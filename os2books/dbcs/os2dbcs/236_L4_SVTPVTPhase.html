SVT/PVT Phase

This is the final test for the software product. All the supported hardware combinations are required to test in this phase, especially the DBCS unique system and peripheral hardwares. You can use the SBCS system softwares or hardwares to develop a DBCS software product, but in the testing, especially the final testing, the customer equivalent environment is required.
