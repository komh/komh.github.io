AddrS Field - AddrType

    AddrType (BYTE)

      Type of address specified.

      Type of address specified. Valid types are:

      EXPR_TYPE_SEG (0x01)

        Address is of form segment:offset.
      EXPR_TYPE_LIN (0x02)
        Address type is linear.
      EXPR_TYPE_SEL (0x09)
        Address is of form selector:offset.
      EXPR_TYPE_PHY (0x0A)
        Address type is physical.
