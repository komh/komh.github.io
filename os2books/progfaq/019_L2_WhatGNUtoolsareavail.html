What GNU tools are available and where can I find them?

Nearly all the GNU utilities have been ported to OS/2 2.x - and nearly all of those ports are located on cdrom.com in os2/2_x/unix/gnu. Other, more involved (or independent) ports of GNU software is scattered about cdrom.com, including a PM version of GhostView and GhostScript.
