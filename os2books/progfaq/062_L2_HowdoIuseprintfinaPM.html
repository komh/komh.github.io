How do I use printf() in a PM program?

Use PMPRINTF, check your local BBS's, CIS, BIX or cdrom.com or redirect stdout and stderr to files. 

    // Redirect 'stderr'.
    (void) freopen ("stderr.txt", "w", stderr) ;

    // Redirect 'stdout'.
    (void) freopen ("stdout.txt", "w", stdout) ;
