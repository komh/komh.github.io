DosDevConfig

EXTRN  DosDevConfig:FAR
INCL_DOSDEVICES     EQU 1

PUSH@  OTHER   DeviceInfo    ;Requested information (returned)
PUSH   WORD    Item          ;Item number
PUSH   WORD    Parm          ;Reserved (must be zero)
CALL   DosDevConfig

Returns WORD
