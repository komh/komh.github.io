EXTRN  DosWrite:FAR
INCL_DOSFILEMGR     EQU 1

PUSH   WORD    FileHandle    ;File handle
PUSH@  OTHER   BufferArea    ;User buffer
PUSH   WORD    BufferLength  ;Buffer length
PUSH@  WORD    BytesWritten  ;Bytes written (returned)
CALL   DosWrite

Returns WORD
