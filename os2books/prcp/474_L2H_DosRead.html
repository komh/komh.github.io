DosRead

EXTRN  DosRead:FAR
INCL_DOSFILEMGR     EQU 1

PUSH   WORD    FileHandle    ;File Handle
PUSH@  OTHER   BufferArea    ;User buffer (returned)
PUSH   WORD    BufferLength  ;Buffer length
PUSH@  WORD    BytesRead     ;Bytes read (returned)
CALL   DosRead

Returns WORD
