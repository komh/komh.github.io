EXTRN  DosPeekNmPipe:FAR

PUSH@  WORD    BytesRead     ;Bytes read (returned)
PUSH@  DWORD   BytesAvail    ;Bytes available (returned)
PUSH@  WORD    PipeState     ;Pipe state (returned)
CALL   DosPeekNmPipe
