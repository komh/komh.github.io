DosFileIO

EXTRN DosFileIO:FAR
INCL_DOSFILEMGR     EQU 1

PUSH   WORD  FileHandle      ;File handle
PUSH@  OTHER CommandList     ;Command buffer
PUSH   WORD  CommandListLen  ;Length of command buffer
PUSH@  WORD  ErrorOffset     ;Command error offset (returned)
CALL   DosFileIO

Returns WORD
