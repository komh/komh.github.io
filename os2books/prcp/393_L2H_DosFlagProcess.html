DosFlagProcess

EXTRN  DosFlagProcess:FAR
INCL_DOSSIGNALS     EQU 1

PUSH   WORD    ProcessID     ;Process ID to flag
PUSH   WORD    ActionCode    ;Indicate to flag descendants
PUSH   WORD    Flagnum       ;Flag number
PUSH   WORD    Flagarg       ;Flag argument
CALL   DosFlagProcess

Returns WORD
