DosTimerStart

EXTRN  DosTimerStart:FAR
INCL_DOSDATETIME    EQU 1

PUSH   DWORD   TimeInterval  ;Interval size (in milliseconds)
PUSH   DWORD   SemHandle     ;System semaphore handle
PUSH@  WORD    Handle        ;Timer handle (returned)
CALL   DosTimerStart

Returns WORD
