DosPutMessage

EXTRN  DosPutMessage:FAR
INCL_DOSMISC      EQU 1

PUSH   WORD    FileHandle    ;Handle of output file/device
PUSH   WORD    MessageLength ;Length of message buffer
PUSH@  OTHER   MessageBuffer ;Message buffer
CALL   DosPutMessage

Returns WORD
