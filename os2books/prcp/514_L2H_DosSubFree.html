DosSubFree

EXTRN  DosSubFree:FAR
INCL_DOSMEMMGR      EQU 1

PUSH   WORD    SegSelector   ;Segment selector
PUSH   WORD    BlockOffset   ;Offset of memory block to free
PUSH   WORD    Size          ;Size of block in bytes
CALL   DosSubFree

Returns WORD
