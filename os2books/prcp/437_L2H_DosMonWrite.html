DosMonWrite

EXTRN  DosMonWrite:FAR
INCL_DOSMONITORS    EQU 1

PUSH@  OTHER   BufferO       ;Monitor output buffer
PUSH@  OTHER   DataBuffer    ;Buffer from which records are taken
PUSH   WORD    Bytecnt       ;Number of bytes
CALL   DosMonWrite

Returns WORD
