VioWrtTTY

EXTRN  VioWrtTTY:FAR
INCL_VIO            EQU 1

PUSH@  OTHER   CharStr       ;String to be written
PUSH   WORD    Length        ;Length of string
PUSH   WORD    VioHandle     ;Video handle
CALL   VioWrtTTY

Returns WORD
