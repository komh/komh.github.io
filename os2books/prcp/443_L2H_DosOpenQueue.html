DosOpenQueue

EXTRN  DosOpenQueue:FAR
INCL_DOSQUEUES      EQU 1

PUSH@  WORD    OwnerPID      ;Queue owners' PID (returned)
PUSH@  WORD    QueueHandle   ;Queue handle (returned)
PUSH@  ASCIIZ  QueueName     ;Queue name string
CALL   DosOpenQueue

Returns WORD
