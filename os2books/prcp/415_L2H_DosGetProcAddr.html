DosGetProcAddr

EXTRN  DosGetProcAddr:FAR
INCL_DOSMODULEMGR   EQU 1

PUSH   WORD    ModuleHandle  ;Module handle
PUSH@  ASCIIZ  ProcName      ;Module name string
PUSH@  DWORD   ProcAddress   ;Procedure address (returned)
CALL   DosGetProcAddr

Returns WORD
