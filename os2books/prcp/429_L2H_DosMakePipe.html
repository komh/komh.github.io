DosMakePipe

EXTRN  DosMakePipe:FAR
INCL_DOSQUEUES      EQU 1

PUSH@  WORD    ReadHandle    ;Read handle (returned)
PUSH@  WORD    WriteHandle   ;Write handle (returned)
PUSH   WORD    PipeSize      ;Size to reserve for the pipe
CALL   DosMakePipe

Returns WORD
