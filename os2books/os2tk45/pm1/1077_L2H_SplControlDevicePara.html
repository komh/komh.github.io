SplControlDevice Parameter - ulControl

    ulControl (ULONG) - input

      Operation to perform.

      PRD_DELETE

        Delete current print job
      PRD_PAUSE
        Pause printing
      PRD_CONT
        Continue paused print job
      PRD_RESTART
        Restart print job.
