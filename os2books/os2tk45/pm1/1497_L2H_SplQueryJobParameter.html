SplQueryJob Parameter - pBuf

    pBuf (PVOID) - in/out

      Buffer.

      The returned contents of the buffer depend on the value specify in ulLevel as follow:

        ulLevel

          Buffer Contents
        0
          The job identifier
        2
          A PRJINFO2 structure, with variable information, up to the cbBuf of pBuf
        3
          A PRJINFO3 structure, with variable information, up to the cbBuf of pBuf.
