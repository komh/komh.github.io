svc_destroy()

The svc_destroy() call deletes the RPC service transport handle xprt, which becomes undefined after this routine is called.

Syntax 

#include <rpc\rpc.h>

void
svc_destroy(xprt)
SVCXPRT *xprt;

Parameter

xprt

    Pointer to the service transport handle.
