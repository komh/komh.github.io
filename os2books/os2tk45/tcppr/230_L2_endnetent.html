endnetent()

The endnetent() call closes the ETC\NETWORKS file, which contains information about known networks.

Note: Calls made to the getnetent(), getnetbyaddr(), or getnetbyname() call open the ETC\NETWORKS file.

Syntax 

#include <netdb.h>
void endnetent()
