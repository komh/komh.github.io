Decompression

The following section illustrates how to decompress an image to play motion video. Follow these steps for decompression support:

  • Open the file and determine which CODEC(s) are required.
  • Load the CODEC DLL file.
  • Load and initialize the CODEC procedure.
  • Call the CODEC to decompress data.
  • Close the CODEC and release resources after use.
