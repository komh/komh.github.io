event

  • An occurrence of significance to a task; for example, an SNMP trap, the opening of a window or a submap, or the completion of an asynchronous operation.

  • In the NetView and NETCENTER programs, a record indicating irregularities of operation in physical elements of a network.

  • In the Desktop Management Interface (DMI), a type of indication (unsolicited report) that originates from a component instrumentation. See also indication.
