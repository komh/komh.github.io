ALIGN (Align Code or Data Item)

Advances the current location counter to the next byte boundary that is a multiple of Expression.

    Syntax 

      ALIGN Expression
    Example

      To align to a 2-byte boundary: 

      ALIGN 2

      To align to a 4-byte boundary: 

      ALIGN 4
