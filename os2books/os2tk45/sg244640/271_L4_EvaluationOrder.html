Evaluation Order

Expression are evaluated left to right by applying the following order of precedence to operators, separators and in-built functions:

  • & # % %% _ ! NOT SEG OFF BY WO DW POI PORT WPORT

  • MOD

  • > < >= <=

  • AND XOR OR

  • && ||
