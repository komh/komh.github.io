Drive Parameter Block for OS/2 Warp V4.0 and OS/2 Warp V3.0

    Pointers

      VPB field vpi_pDPB points to the associated DPB.
    Locations
      DPB segment is dynamically allocated from the kernel resident heap.
    VM Owner
      dpb (0xff96).
