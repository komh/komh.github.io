Physical Arena Information Structures

    Pointers

      SAS_vm_prt
    Locations
      System Arena.

      Two PAIs exist as part of the kernel load module. These are located at labels _pgPageablePAI and _pgResidentPAI.

    VM Owners
      os2krnl (0xffaa)
