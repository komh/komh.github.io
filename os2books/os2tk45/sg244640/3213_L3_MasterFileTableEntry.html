Pointers

    SFT field sf_MFT points to the associated MFT entry.

    SAS field SAS_file_MFT points to the PTREE head for the MFT PTREE.

Locations
    Dynamically allocated from the kernel resident heap.
VM Owner
    mft (0xff9e).
