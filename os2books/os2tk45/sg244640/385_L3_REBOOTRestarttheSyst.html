.REBOOT - Restart the System

Restart the system.

Syntax: 


────.REBOOT ──────────────────────────────────────────────────

Parameters:

None

Results & Notes:

DosHlp service DosHlpReboot is called to restart the system. No system shutdown processing, whatsoever, is performed.

This command is not available to the Dump Formatter!
