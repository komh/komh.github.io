Translating a Graphics Object

If Tx is equal to 8 and Ty is equal to 5, then:

x' = x + 8

y' = y + 5

The original five points are transformed: (0,0) ─ (8,5)
(0,4) ─ (8,9)
(0,6) ─ (8,11)
(2,4) ─ (10,9)
(2,6) ─ (10,11)
