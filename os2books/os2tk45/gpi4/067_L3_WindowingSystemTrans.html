Windowing-System Transformation

There is one transformation, the windowing-system transformation, performed automatically by the PM. The windowing-system transformation, which is a translation transformation only, maps the coordinates of the picture in device space to the coordinates of the screen window or printer page. This happens when a picture is first drawn and whenever the display window in which the picture has been drawn is moved.
