set_exception - Remarks

The set_exception method returns an exception to the client. The major parameter can have one of three possible values:

    NO_EXCEPTION

      Indicates a normal outcome of the operation. It is not necessary to invoke set_exception to indicate a normal outcome; it is the default behavior if the method simply returns.
    USER_EXCEPTION
      Indicates a user-defined exception.
    SYSTEM_EXCEPTION
      Indicates a system-defined exception.
