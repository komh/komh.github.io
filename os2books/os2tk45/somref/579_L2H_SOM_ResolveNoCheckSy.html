SOM_ResolveNoCheck - Syntax

This macro obtains a pointer to a method procedure, without doing consistency checks. 

SOMObject        objPtr;
<token>    className;
<token>    methodName;
somMethodPtr     rc;

rc = SOM_ResolveNoCheck(objPtr, className,
       methodName);
