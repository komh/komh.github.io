SOM_Resolve - Syntax

This macro obtains a pointer to a method procedure.

Note: For backward compatibility, this method does not take an Environment parameter. 

SOMObject        objPtr;
<token>    className;
<token>    methodName;
RC               somMethod;

somMethod = SOM_Resolve(objPtr, className,
              methodName);
