Example

This example creates a push-button control with the WS_TABSTOP and WS_VISIBLE styles. 

  CONTROL "OK", 101, 10, 10, 20, 50, WC_BUTTON, BS_PUSHBUTTON |
                                                WS_TABSTOP    |
                                                WS_VISIBLE
