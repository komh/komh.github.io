Example

The SUBITEMSIZE statement in this example specifies that each HELPSUBITEM statement contains three words. 

HELPSUBTABLE 1
SUBITEMSIZE 3
BEGIN
    HELPSUBITEM IDCLD_FILEMENU, IDHP_FILEMENU, 5
    HELPSUBITEM IDCLD_HELPMENU, IDHP_HELPMENU, 6
END
