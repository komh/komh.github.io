wpQueryLockupBackground Parameter - pulImageMode

    pulImageMode (ULONG *) - in/out

      Pointer to one of the following image modes:

      BACKGROUND_NORMAL_IMAGE

        Normal image.
      BACKGROUND_TILED_IMAGE
        Tiled image.
      BACKGROUND_SCALED_IMAGE
        Scaled image.
