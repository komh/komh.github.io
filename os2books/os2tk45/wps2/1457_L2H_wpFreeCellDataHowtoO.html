wpFreeCellData - How to Override

This method must be overridden in any subclass of WPPalette that allocates storage associated with a palette cell.

This method should be overriden by all subclasses that wish to save and restore part of their cell data. For example, the scheme palette will save the name of the background bitmap for the scheme.
