wpSetFldrAttr Parameter - ulView

    ulView (ULONG) - input

      Flag indicating an object's open view.

      OPEN_CONTENTS

        Open contents view.
      OPEN_DETAILS
        Open details view.
      OPEN_TREE
        Open tree view.
