wpQueryFldrAttr Parameter - ulView

    ulView (ULONG) - input

      Flag indicating the view to query.

      OPEN_CONTENTS

        Open contents view.
      OPEN_DETAILS
        Open details view.
      OPEN_HELP
        Display HelpPanel.
      OPEN_TREE
        Open tree view.
