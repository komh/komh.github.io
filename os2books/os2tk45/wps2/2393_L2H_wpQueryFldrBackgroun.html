wpQueryFldrBackground Parameter - pulBackgroundType

    pulBackgroundType (ULONG *) - output

      The background type.

      BACKGROUND_COLOR_ONLY

        The background is color only (no image).
      BACKGROUND_IMAGE
        The background is an image.
