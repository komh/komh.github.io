wpModifyMenu Parameter - ulView

    ulView (ULONG) - input

      The view that the menu is being invoked on.

      OPEN_CONTENTS

        Open contents view.
      OPEN_TREE
        Open tree view.
      OPEN_DETAILS
        Open details view.
      CLOSED_ICON
        Closed icon view.
