wpSetLockupBackground Parameter - ulImageMode

    ulImageMode (ULONG) - input

      The following are the image modes:

      BACKGROUND_NORMAL_IMAGE

        Normal image.
      BACKGROUND_TILED_IMAGE
        Tiled image.
      BACKGROUND_SCALED_IMAGE
        Scaled image.
