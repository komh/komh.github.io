wpSetFldrBackground Parameter - ulImageMode

    ulImageMode (ULONG) - output

      The image mode.

      BACKGROUND_NORMAL_IMAGE

        Normal image.
      BACKGROUND_TILED_IMAGE
        Tiled image.
      BACKGROUND_SCALED_IMAGE
        Scaled image.
