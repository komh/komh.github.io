GCHSTE/GCCHSTE Parameter - FLAGS

    FLAGS

      Extra functions:

      RECT (GBIT1)

        Values:

      • Do not draw background rectangle
      • Draw background rectangle.
      CLIP (GBIT1)
        Values:

      • Do not clip to rectangle
      • Clip to rectangle.
      RES1 (GBIT1)
        Reserved value, must be 0.
      LVCP (GBIT1)
        Values:

      • Move current position
      • Leave current position.
      RES2 (GBIT4)
        Reserved value, must be 0.
