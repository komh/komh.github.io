FOCAMETRICS Field - szFamilyname[32]

    szFamilyname[32] (CHAR)

      Font family name.

      The family name defines the basic appearance of the font; for example, "Times New Roman."** This string is null terminated and is, therefore, limited to 32 characters in length.
