    LEN (GLENGTH1)

      Length of following data.

    CELLX (GROSOL)

      X part of marker cell-size attribute.

      Y part of marker cell-size attribute.

      This is an optional extension.

      Values:

      NOTDEFLT (GBIT1)

          Reserved value, must be 0.
