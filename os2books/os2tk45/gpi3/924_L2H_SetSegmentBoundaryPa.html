    MASK

        Reserved value, must be 0.
      XL (GBIT1)
        X left limit.

          Y bottom limit.

        • Not included in list of BB values
        • Is included in list of BB values.
        YT (GBIT1)
          Y top limit.

        • Not included in list of BB values
        • Is included in list of BB values.
        RES2 (GBIT2)
          Reserved value, must be 0.

          Boundary values.
