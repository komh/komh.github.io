FACENAMEDESC Field - usWeightClass

    usWeightClass (USHORT)

      Weight class.

      Indicates the visual weight (thickness of strokes) of the characters in the font:

      FWEIGHT_DONT_CARE

        Any font weight satisfies the request.
      FWEIGHT_ULTRA_LIGHT
        Ultra-light.
      FWEIGHT_EXTRA_LIGHT
        Extra-light.
      FWEIGHT_LIGHT
        Light.
      FWEIGHT_SEMI_LIGHT
        Semi-light.
      FWEIGHT_NORMAL
        Medium (normal) weight.
      FWEIGHT_SEMI_BOLD
        Semi-bold.
      FWEIGHT_BOLD
        Bold.
      FWEIGHT_EXTRA_BOLD
        Extra-bold.
      FWEIGHT_ULTRA_BOLD
        Ultra-bold.
