GSTM/GPSTM Parameter - FLAGS

    FLAGS

      Values:

      RES (GBIT6)

        Reserved value, must be 0.
      CM (GBIT2)
        Matrix control bits:

        B"00"

          Unity matrix
        B"01"
          Concatenate after
        B"10"
          Concatenate before
        B"11"
          Overwrite.
