GSCD/GPSCD Parameter - DIRECTION

    DIRECTION (GBIT8)

      Value for character direction:

      All other values are reserved.

      0x00

        Drawing default
      0x01
        Left to right
      0x02
        Top to bottom
      0x03
        Right to left
      0x04
        Bottom to top.
