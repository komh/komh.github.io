Call Segment - Parameters

    LEN (GLENGTH1)

      Length of following data.

      0x06

        Only valid value.

      RES (GBIT16)

        Reserved value, must be 0.

      SEGNAME (GLONG)

        Name of segment that is to be called.

        The name cannot be 0.
