FOCAMETRICS Field - yExternalLeading

    yExternalLeading (SHORT)

      External leading.

      The amount of guaranteed white space advised by the font designer to appear between adjacent rows of text. This value may be zero (0).

      Note: The fonts built into Presentation Manager have zero (0) in this field.
