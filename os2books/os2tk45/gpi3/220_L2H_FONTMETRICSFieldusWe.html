FONTMETRICS Field - usWeightClass

    usWeightClass (USHORT)

      Weight class.

      Indicates the visual weight (thickness of strokes) of the characters in the font:

        Value

          Description
        1
          Ultra-light
        2
          Extra-light
        3
          Light
        4
          Semi-light
        5
          Medium (normal)
        6
          Semi-bold
        7
          Bold
        8
          Extra-bold
        9
          Ultra-bold
