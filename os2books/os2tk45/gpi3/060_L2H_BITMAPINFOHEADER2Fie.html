BITMAPINFOHEADER2 Field - usUnits

    usUnits (USHORT)

      Units of measure.

      Units of measure of the horizontal and vertical resolution, cxResolution and cyResolution.

      BRU_METRIC

        Pels per meter. This is the default value.
