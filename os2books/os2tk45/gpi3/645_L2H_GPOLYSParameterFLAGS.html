GPOLYS Parameter - FLAGS

    FLAGS

      Internal flags.

      INSIDE (GBIT1)

        Mode shading:

      • Alternate mode.
      • Winding mode.
      MODEL (GBIT1)
        Drawing model:

      • The fill is inclusive of bottom right.
      • The fill is exclusive of bottom right.
      RES2 (GBIT6)
        Reserved value, must be 0.
