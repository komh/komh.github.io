FOCAMETRICS Field - sCharRot

    sCharRot (SHORT)

      Character rotation.

      The rotation of the character glyphs with respect to the baseline, the angle increasing counterclockwise. This is the angle assigned by the font designer.

      Note: The units for this metric are degrees and minutes, encoded as shown in sCharSlope.
