FOCAMETRICS Field - fsCapabilities

    fsCapabilities (SHORT)

      Capabilities.

      This attribute applies only to device fonts.

      FM_CAP_NOMIX

        Characters may not be mixed with graphics.
      QUALITY
        The most significant byte may contain the following numeric value: 0
          Undefined
        1
          DP quality
        2
          DP draft
        3
          Near Letter Quality
        4
          Letter Quality
