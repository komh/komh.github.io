Begin Area - Parameters

    FLAGS

      Internal flags.

      RES1 (GBIT1)

        Reserved for migration:

      • Only valid value.
      BOUNDARY (GBIT1)
        Boundary-line draw indicator:

      • Do not draw boundary lines
      • Draw boundary lines.
      INSIDE (GBIT1)
        Mode shading:

      • Alternate mode
      • Winding mode.
      RES2 (GBIT5)
        Reserved value, must be 0.
