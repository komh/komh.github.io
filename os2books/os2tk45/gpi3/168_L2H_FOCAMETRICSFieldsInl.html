FOCAMETRICS Field - sInlineDir

    sInlineDir (SHORT)

      Inline direction.

      The direction in which the characters in the font are designed for viewing. Shown in degrees, increasing clockwise from the horizontal (left-to-right). Characters are added to a line of text in the inline direction.

      Note: The units for this metric are degrees and minutes, encoded as shown in sCharSlope.
