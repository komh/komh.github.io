Sharp Fillet at Given Position/Sharp Fillet at Current Position - Syntax

This order generates a curve that starts at a given position, and uses points P1 and P2, together with the sharpness specification S1.

Sharp Fillet at Given Position (GSFLT)
X'E4' (LEN, P0, P1, P2, P3, P4, PN-1, PN, S1, S2, SN/2)

Sharp Fillet at Current Position (GCSFLT)
X'A4' (LEN, P1, P2, P3, P4, PN-1, PN, S1, S2, SN/2)
