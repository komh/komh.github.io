CAPABILITY (Waveaudio Command) Keyword - FORMAT

    FORMAT

      Indicates that waveaudio format will be queried. If FORMAT is specified, BITSPERSAMPLE, SAMPLESPERSEC, TAG, and CHANNELS must also be specified.
