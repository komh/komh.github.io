POWER_GETPOWERINFO (62h) - BIOSFlags

    BIOSFlags

      BIOSFlags is a bit mask field with the bit definitions and setting as queried at system initialization. For specific bit definitions, consult the appropriate Advanced Power Management BIOS technical reference materials for the system unit.
