OEMHLP_PCI (0Bh) - Hardware Mechanism

    Hardware Mechanism

      PCI BIOS hardware platform support.

      BIT 0

        Hardware Configuration Mechanism #1
      BIT 1
        Hardware Configuration Mechanism #2
      BIT 2-3
        Reserved (0)
      BIT 4
        Special Cycles supported. HW Mechanism #1
      BIT 5
        Special Cycles supported. HW Mechanism #2
