POWER_SENDPOWEREVENT (40h) - ReturnCode

    ReturnCode

      The function-specific completion value. The valid return values are:

      PowerNoError

        (0000H)
      PowerBadSubId
        (0001H)
      PowerBadReserved
        (0002H)
      PowerBadDevId
        (0003H)
      PowerBadPwrState
        (0004H)
      PowerDisabled
        (0009H)
