TOUCH_DEVINDEP_SETEVENTMASK (53h) - Remarks

The Touch Display device driver gets the new mask for enabled/disabled Touch Display device events from the caller's parameter packet. The mask determines which events are to be queued (that is, readable) by calling TOUCH_DEVDEP_GETLOWPASSFILTER.
