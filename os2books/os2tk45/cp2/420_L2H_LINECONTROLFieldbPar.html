LINECONTROL Field - bParity

    bParity (BYTE)

      Parity flag.

      0x00

        No parity
      0x01
        Odd parity
      0x02
        Even parity (initial value)
      0x03
        Mark parity (parity bit always 1)
      0x04
        Space parity (parity bit always 0)
      0x05-0xFF
        Reserved
