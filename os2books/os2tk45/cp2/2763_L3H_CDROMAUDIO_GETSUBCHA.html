CDROMAUDIO_GETSUBCHANNELQ (63h) - Returns

    02H

      Device not ready. The drive is not ready, or the controller does not respond.
    07H
      Unknown media. The media is a compact disc that is not formatted according to CD Redbook specification.
    10H
      Uncertain media.
    13H
      Unsupported parameter. The first 4 bytes of the parameter buffer are not "CD01".
