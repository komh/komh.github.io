TOUCH_DEVDEP_GETTOUCHTHRESHOLD (63h) - Push hysteresis threshold

    Push hysteresis threshold

      Is the value of Z, when Z is decreasing on the chosen Z scale, at which the Touch display resets the selection detection bit in the status returned by Function 53h.
