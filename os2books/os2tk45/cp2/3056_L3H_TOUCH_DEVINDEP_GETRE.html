TOUCH_DEVINDEP_GETREADEVENTQUEUE (66h) - Remarks

Bit definitions of Event Status are identical to those of the Event Mask emulation state. (See also TOUCH_DEVINDEP_SETEVENTMASK and TOUCH_DEVINDEP_GETEVENTMASK.)
