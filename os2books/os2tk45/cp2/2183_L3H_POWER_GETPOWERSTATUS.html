POWER_GETPOWERSTATUS (60h) - BatteryLife

    BatteryLife

      Contains the remaining power level for the DC battery power source. Supported values are:

    • to 100
        = Percentage of battery life remaining
      255
        = Unknown
      All other values are reserved.
