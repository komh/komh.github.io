MOU_UPDATEDISPLAYMODE (51h) - Address of Extended Data Area

    Address of Extended Data Area

      The Far-16 address to an extended-mode data structure, or zero, if none. The format of the extended-mode data structure is determined by the physical device driver and is unknown to the operating system.
