POWER_GETPOWEREVENT (61h) - ReturnCode

    ReturnCode

      The function-specific completion value. The valid return values are:

      PowerNoError

        (0000H)
      PowerBadLength
        (0008H)
      PowerNoEventQueue
        (000AH)
      PowerQueueOverflow
        (000EH)
