ASYNC_SETLINECTRL (42h) - Parity

    Parity

      Has the following value and meaning:

      00h

        No parity
      01h
        Odd parity
      02h
        Even parity (initial value)
      03h
        Mark parity (parity bit always 1)
      04h
        Space parity (parity bit always 0)
      05h-FFh
        Reserved
