TOUCH_DEVDEP_SETEMULXY (57h) - Remarks

Calls to the mouse device-independent driver by way of the IDC interface will have the X and Y coordinate offsets applied to the touch X, Y, Z data. Data supplied to the Touch device-independent driver is not affected.
