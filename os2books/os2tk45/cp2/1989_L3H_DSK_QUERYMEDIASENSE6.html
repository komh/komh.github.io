DSK_QUERYMEDIASENSE (60h) - Media Sense Information

    Media Sense Information

      On return, this field can be interpreted as follows:

    • Unable to determine media type
    • 720KB diskette is present in 3.5-inch drive
    • 44MB diskette is present in 3.5-inch drive
    • 88MB diskette is present in 3.5-inch drive
