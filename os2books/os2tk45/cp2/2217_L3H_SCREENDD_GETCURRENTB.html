SCREENDD_GETCURRENTBANK (00h) - Packet Length

    Packet Length

      Represents the combined length of all parameter packet fields. This is a required field for all calls, including those made by way of the 32-bit DosDevIOCtl, with a minimum packet length of 10 bytes. If the verification of the packet field fails, the ERROR_124_INVALID_PARAMETER message is returned.
