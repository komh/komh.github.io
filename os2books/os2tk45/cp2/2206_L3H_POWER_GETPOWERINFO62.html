POWER_GETPOWERINFO (62h) - BIOSVersion

    BIOSVersion

      BIOSVersion is a complex field composed of the following BYTE values:

      • The high BYTE contains the Advanced Power Management BIOS major revision level value.

      • The low BYTE contains the Advanced Power Management BIOS minor revision level value.
      A combined WORD value of 0 indicates that no Advanced Power Management BIOS support is active or available. For the meaning of a specific version number, consult the appropriate BIOS technical reference materials for the system unit.
