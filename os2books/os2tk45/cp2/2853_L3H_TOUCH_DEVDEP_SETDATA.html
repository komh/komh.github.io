TOUCH_DEVDEP_SETDATAREPORTRATE (58h) - Report rate

    Report rate

      Sets the maximum rate at which data packets are returned by the Touch display while the touch contact force exceeds the On-Screen threshold (see TOUCH_DEVDEP_SETTOUCHTHRESHOLD.) The values are:

    • 10 reports per second

    • 20 reports per second

    • 30 reports per second

    • 40 reports per second

    • 60 reports per second

    • Driver default value

      All other values are reserved.
