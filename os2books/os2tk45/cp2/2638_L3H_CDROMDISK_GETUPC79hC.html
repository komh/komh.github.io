CDROMDISK_GETUPC (79h) - Control ADR

    Control ADR

      The Track-Control Information byte corresponds to the byte in the TOC lead-in track containing the two 4-bit control field and ADR. The Control information is in the most-significant 4 bits, and the ADR information is in the least-significant 4 bits.
