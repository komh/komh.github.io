LINECONTROL Field - bStopBits

    bStopBits (BYTE)

      Stop bit flag.

      0x00

        1 stop bit (initial value)
      0x01
        1.5 stop bits (valid with 5-bit WORD length only)
      0x02
        2 stop bits (not valid with 5-bit WORD length)
      0x03-0xFF
        Reserved
