TOUCH_DEVDEP_SETTOUCHTHRESHOLD (56h) - On-Screen threshold

    On-Screen threshold

      Sets the value of Z, on the chosen Z scale, at which the Touch display interprets the touch contact force as On-screen and starts reporting data. Data is reported only while the force exceeds this threshold.
