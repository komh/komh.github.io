CDROMDISK_DEVICESTATUS (60h) - Returns

    02h

      Device not ready: The drive is not ready, or the controller does not respond.
    13h
      Unsupported parameter: The addressing mode is out of range, or the first 4 bytes of the parameter packet are not "Signature".
