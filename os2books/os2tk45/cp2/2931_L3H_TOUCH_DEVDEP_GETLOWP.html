TOUCH_DEVDEP_GETLOWPASSFILTER (66h) - Filter frequency

    Filter frequency

      Indicates the frequency cut-off value for the filter:

    • Default (Medium)

    • Slow

    • Medium

    • Fast

    • Rapid

      All other values are reserved.
