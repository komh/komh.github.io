CDROMAUDIO_SETCHANNELCTRL (40h) - Returns

    02H

      Device not ready: The drive is not ready, or the controller does not respond.
    07H
      Unknown media: The media is a compact disc that is not formatted according to the CD Redbook specifications.
    13H
      Unsupported parameter: Any of the input channels are out of range, or the first 4 bytes of the parameter buffer are not "CD01."
