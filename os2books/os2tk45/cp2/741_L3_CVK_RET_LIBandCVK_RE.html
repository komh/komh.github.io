CVK_RET_LIB and CVK_RET_KIL Events

┌──────────────────────────────┬──────────┬──────────────────────────────┐
│Event                         │Code      │Description                   │
├──────────────────────────────┼──────────┼──────────────────────────────┤
│KDP_T_LINK                    │102       │Module loaded                 │
├──────────────────────────────┼──────────┼──────────────────────────────┤
│KDP_T_UNLINK                  │103       │Module unloaded               │
└──────────────────────────────┴──────────┴──────────────────────────────┘

Fields returned in cvkcmd_s are as follows:

    Cmd

      CVK_RET_LIB or CVK_RET_KIL
    Value
      MTE handle of module in question.
    OffV
      Not used.
    SegV
      Slot number of thread.
    MTE
      MTE entry of executable running in process.
    PID
      PID of process that generated the event.
    TID
      TID of thread that generated the event.
    DBit
      Not used.
    UCHAR NAME[ ]
      Null-terminated full pathname of module in question (immediately follows DBit and overlays Reg and MemCache)
