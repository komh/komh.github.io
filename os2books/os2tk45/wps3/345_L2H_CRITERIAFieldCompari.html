CRITERIA Field - ComparisonType

    ComparisonType (ULONG)

      The comparison type.

      May be one of the following values:

      • CMP_EQUAL
      • CMP_GREATER
      • CMP_LESS
      • CMP_GREATER_OR_EQUAL
      • CMP_LESS_OR_EQUAL
      • CMP_NO_EQUAL
