MMAUDIOHEADER Field - ulContentType

    ulContentType (ULONG)

      Audio content type. Acceptable values for this field are as follows:

      MMIO_MIDI_UNKNOWN

        Unknown MIDI content.
      MMIO_MIDI_VOICE
        Limited range.
      MMIO_MIDI_MUSIC
        FM radio or equivalent.
      MMIO_MIDI_HIFI
        High quality recording.
