BITMAPINFOHEADER2 Field - cyResolution

    cyResolution (ULONG)

      Vertical resolution Y component of the target device that the bitmap is intended for, in the units specified by the usUnits field. This information, along with the X resolution, enables applications to pick out a bitmap from a resource group to best fit the characteristics of the current output device.
