MMIMAGEHEADER Field - ulContentType

    ulContentType (ULONG)

      The image content type. The acceptable values for this field are as follows:

      MMIO_IMAGE_UNKNOWN

        Unknown image content.
      MMIO_IMAGE_DRAWING
        Simple drawing.
      MMIO_IMAGE_GRAPH
        Graphs and cartoons.
      MMIO_IMAGE_PHOTO
        Varying color and shades.
