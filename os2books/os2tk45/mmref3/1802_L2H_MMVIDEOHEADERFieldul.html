MMVIDEOHEADER Field - ulContentType

    ulContentType (ULONG)

      The video content type is either MMIO_VIDEO_DATA or MMIO_VIDEO_UNKNOWN. MMIO_VIDEO_DATA is a recognized video type, MMIO_VIDEO_UNKNOWN is not supported.
