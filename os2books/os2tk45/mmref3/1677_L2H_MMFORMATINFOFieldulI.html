MMFORMATINFO Field - ulIOProcType

    ulIOProcType (ULONG)

      Specifies the type of I/O procedure. These include:

        MMIO_IOPROC_STORAGESYSTEM
        MMIO_IOPROC_FILEFORMAT
        MMIO_IOPROC_DATAFORMAT
