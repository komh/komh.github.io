WAVE_HEADER Field - usChannels

    usChannels (USHORT)

      Number of channels represented in the waveform data; Channel 1 for monaural and Channel 2 for stereo.

      CH_1

        Mono
      CH_2
        Stereo
      CH_3
        Quad
