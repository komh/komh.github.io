MMMIDIHEADER Field - ulContentType

    ulContentType (ULONG)

      This field contains the type of MIDI content and it has the following possible values:

      MMIO_MIDI_UNKNOWN

        Unknown MIDI content.
      MMIO_MIDI_VOICE
        Limited range.
      MMIO_MIDI_MUSIC
        FM radio or equivalent.
      MMIO_MIDI_HIFI
        High quality recording.
