CODECINIFILEINFO Field - ulMediaType

    ulMediaType (ULONG)

      Indicates the media type of the CODEC Proc (for example, image, audio, video or plain data). Following are the currently defined types:

      MMIO_MEDIATYPE_DIGITALVIDEO

        Video media type.
