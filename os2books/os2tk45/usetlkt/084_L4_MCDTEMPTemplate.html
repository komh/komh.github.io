MCDTEMP Template

MCDTEMP (located in \TOOLKIT\SAMPLES\MM\MCDTEMP) provides a basic template to write a media control driver (MCD). Refer to the \ADMCT and \CDMCIDRV subdirectories for specific streaming or to the multimedia I/O (MMIO) samples.
