GpiPolySpline Parameter - aptlPoints

    aptlPoints (PPOINTL) - input

      An array of points.

      The points are given in this order: 

      c11, c12, e1, c21, c22, e2, ... cs1, cs2, es

      where:

      cs1

        is the first control point of spline s
      cs2
        is the second control point of spline s
      es
        is the end point of spline s.
