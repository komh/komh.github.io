GpiSetLineEnd - Remarks

The line-end attribute defines the shape of the ends of lines or arcs at the beginning and end of an open figure. This attribute is used only in the GpiModifyPath function (with a lMode parameter of MPATH_STROKE) or in the GpiStrokePath function.

The attribute mode (see GpiSetAttrMode) determines whether the current value of the line-end attribute is preserved.
