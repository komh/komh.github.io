        Detectability
      ATTR_VISIBLE
        Visibility
      ATTR_CHAINED
        Chained
      ATTR_DYNAMIC
        Dynamic
      ATTR_FASTCHAIN
        Fast chaining

        Propagate detectability
      ATTR_PROP_VISIBLE
        Propagate visibility.
