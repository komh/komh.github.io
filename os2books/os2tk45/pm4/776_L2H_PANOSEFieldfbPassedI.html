PANOSE Field - fbPassedISO

    fbPassedISO (BYTE)

      Font passed ISO test.

      The following flags indicate those displays and resolutions at which the font complied with ISO 9241:

      FM_ISO_9518_640

      FM_ISO_9515_640
      FM_ISO_9515_1024
      FM_ISO_9517_640
      FM_ISO_9517_1024
