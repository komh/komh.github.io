HCINFO Field - flAttributes

    flAttributes (LONG)

      Attributes of the form identifier.

      HCAPS_SELECTABLE

        The form is installed on the printer as given by the printer properties dialog. It is available from an alternate form source without operator intervention. If the form does not have this bit set and is used (if the user selects it), a "forms mismatch" error is generated by the printer object.
      HCAPS_CURRENT
        The form is the one currently selected by the pdriv field (the job properties) in DEVOPENSTRUC
