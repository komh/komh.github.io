FIELDINFOINSERT Field - pFieldInfoOrder

    pFieldInfoOrder (PFIELDINFO)

      Column order.

      Orders the FIELDINFO structure or structures relative to other FIELDINFO structures in the container. The values can be:

      CMA_FIRST

        Places a FIELDINFO structure, or list of FIELDINFO structures, at the front of the list of columns.
      CMA_END
        Places a FIELDINFO structure, or list of FIELDINFO structures, at the end of the list of columns.
      Other
        Pointer to a FIELDINFO structure that this structure, or list of structures, is to be inserted after.
