SWCNTRL Field - uchVisibility

    uchVisibility (ULONG)

      Visibility:

      SWL_VISIBLE

        Visible in startup list
      SWL_INVISIBLE
        Invisible in startup list
      SWL_GRAYED
        Item cannot be switched to (note that it is not actually grayed in the list).
