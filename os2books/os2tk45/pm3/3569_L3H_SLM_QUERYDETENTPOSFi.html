SLM_QUERYDETENTPOS Field - fDetentLocation

    fDetentLocation (USHORT)

      Scale.

      The scale along which the detent is located. One of the following:

      SMA_SCALE1

        Detent position is along scale 1.
      SMA_SCALE2
        Detent position is along scale 2.
