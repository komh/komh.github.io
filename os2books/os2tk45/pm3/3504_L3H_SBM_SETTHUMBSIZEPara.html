SBM_SETTHUMBSIZE - Parameters

    svisible (SHORT)

      Size of the visible part of the document.

    stotal (SHORT)

      Size of the entire document.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
