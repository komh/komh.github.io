WM_HSCROLLCLIPBOARD Field - sposScroll

    sposScroll (SHORT)

      Scroll position.

      The position is either:

    • scodeScroll is other than SB_SLIDERPOSITION
      Other
        The position of the slider when scodeScroll is SB_SLIDERPOSITION.
