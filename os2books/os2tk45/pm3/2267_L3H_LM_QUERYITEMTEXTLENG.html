LM_QUERYITEMTEXTLENGTH - Parameters

    sItemIndex (SHORT)

      Item index.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    sTextLength (SHORT)

      Length of item text.

      The length of the text string, excluding the null termination character.

      LIT_ERROR

        Error occurred. For example, the item specified by its index does not exist.
      Other
        Length of item text.
