WM_MOUSEMAP - Parameters

    ulPhysButton (ULONG)

      The physical button number (1, 2, or 3).

    ulMappedButton (ULONG)

      The button to be mapped to (1, 2, or 3).

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.
