MLM_QUERYSEL Field - sMinSel

    sMinSel (SHORT)

      Minimum insertion point of selection.

      This value is rounded down to 65 535, if necessary.

      ReturnCode contains sMinSel and sMaxSel for a usQueryMode of MLFQS_MINMAXSEL.
