DM_EMPHASIZETARGET - Parameters

    sx (SHORT)

      X-coordinate.

      X-coordinate of the pointing device pointer in window coordinates.

    sy (SHORT)

      Y-coordinate.

      Y-coordinate of the pointing device pointer in window coordinates.

    usEmphasis (USHORT)

      Flags.

      TRUE

        Apply emphasis.
      FALSE
        Remove emphasis.

      ulReserved (ULONG)

        Reserved value, should be 0.
