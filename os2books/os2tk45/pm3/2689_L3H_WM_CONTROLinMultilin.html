WM_CONTROL (in Multiline Entry Fields) Field - ulErrInd

    ulErrInd (ULONG)

      Clipboard fail flag.

      param2 contains ulErrInd for a usnotifycode of MLN_CLPBDFAIL.

      MLFCPBD_TOOMUCHTEXT

        Text amount exceeds clipboard capacity
      MLFCPBD_CLPBDERROR
        A clipboard error occurred.
