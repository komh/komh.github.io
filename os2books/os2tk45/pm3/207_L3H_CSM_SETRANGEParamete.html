CSM_SETRANGE - Parameters

    Low (SHORT)

      The minimum value of the circular slider.

    High (SHORT)

      The maximum value of the circular slider.

    rc (ULONG)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
