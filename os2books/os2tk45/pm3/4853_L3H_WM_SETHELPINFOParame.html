WM_SETHELPINFO - Parameters

    lhelpinfo (LONG)

      New help information.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
