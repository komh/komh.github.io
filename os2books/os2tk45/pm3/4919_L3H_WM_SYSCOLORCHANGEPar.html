WM_SYSCOLORCHANGE - Parameters

    flOptions (ULONG)

      Options.

      Copied from the parameter of the WinSetSysColors function and therefore specifies which palette has been changed.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    ulReserved (ULONG)
