WM_PSIZE - Parameters

    scxold (SHORT)

      Old horizontal size.

    scyold (SHORT)

      Old vertical size.

    scxnew (SHORT)

      New horizontal size.

    scynew (SHORT)

      New vertical size.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.
