WM_CONTROLPOINTER - Parameters

    usidCtl (USHORT)

      Control identifier.

    hptrNew (HPOINTER)

      Handle of the pointing device pointer that the control is to use.

    hptrRet (HPOINTER)

      Returned pointing device-pointer handle that is then used by the control.
