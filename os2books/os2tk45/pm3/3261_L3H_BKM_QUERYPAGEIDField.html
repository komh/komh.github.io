BKM_QUERYPAGEID Field - ulPageId

    ulPageId (ULONG)

      Location page ID.

      Page identifier used for locating the requested page. This identifier is ignored if the BKA_FIRST, BKA_LAST, or BKA_TOP attribute is specified.
