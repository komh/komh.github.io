WM_SETBORDERSIZE - Parameters

    uscx (USHORT)

      Width of border.

    uscy (USHORT)

      Height of border.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
