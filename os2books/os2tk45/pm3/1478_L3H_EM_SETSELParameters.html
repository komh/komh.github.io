EM_SETSEL - Parameters

    usminsel (USHORT)

      Offset of the first character in the selection.

    usmaxsel (USHORT)

      Offset of the first character after the selection.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
