WM_SETACCELTABLE - Parameters

    haccelNew (HACCEL)

      New accelerator table.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
