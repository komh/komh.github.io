WM_BUTTON3DOWN Field - fsflags

    fsflags (USHORT)

      Keyboard control codes.

      In addition to the control codes described with the WM_CHAR message, the following keyboard control codes are valid.

      KC_NONE

        Indicates that no key is pressed.
