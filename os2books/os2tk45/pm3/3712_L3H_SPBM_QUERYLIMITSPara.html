SPBM_QUERYLIMITS - Parameters

    plUpLimit (PLONG)

      Pointer to a LONG that will receive the returned upper limit.

    plLowLimit (PLONG)

      Pointer to a LONG that will receive the returned lower limit.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        Error occurred.
