BKM_QUERYTABBITMAP Field - ulPageId

    ulPageId (ULONG)

      Page ID.

      Page identifier of the page whose bit-map handle is requested. This should be a page for which a BKA_MAJOR or BKA_MINOR attribute has been specified.
