WM_JOURNALNOTIFY Field - ulCommand

    ulCommand (ULONG)

      Command to journal.

      JRN_QUEUESTATUS

        The WinQueryQueueStatus command must be journaled.
      JRN_PHYSKEYSTATE
        The WinGetPhysKeyState command must be journaled.
