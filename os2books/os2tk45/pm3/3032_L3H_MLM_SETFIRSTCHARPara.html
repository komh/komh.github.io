MLM_SETFIRSTCHAR - Parameters

    iptFVC (IPT)

      Insertion point to place in top left-hand corner.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.

    rc (BOOL)

      Success indicator.

      TRUE

        Successful completion
      FALSE
        An error occurred.
