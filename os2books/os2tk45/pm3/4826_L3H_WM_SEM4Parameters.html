WM_SEM4 - Parameters

    flAccumBits (ULONG)

      Semaphore value.

      The semaphore values from all the WM_SEM4 messages posted to a queue, are accumulated by a logical-OR operation.

    ulReserved1 (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.
