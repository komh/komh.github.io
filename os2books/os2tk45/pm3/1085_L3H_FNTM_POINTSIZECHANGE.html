FNTM_POINTSIZECHANGED - Parameters

    pPointSize (PSZ)

      Pointer to the text in the point-size entry field.

    fxPointSize (FIXED)

      Point size.

      The fxPointSize field in FONTDLG stated in fixed-point notation.

    ulReserved (ULONG)

      Reserved value, should be 0.
