WinSetMenuItemText - Remarks

This macro expands to: 

#define WinSetMenuItemText (hwndMenu, usId, pszText)
  ((BOOL)WinSendMsg(hwndMenu,
                    MM_SETITEMTEXT,
                    MPFROMSHORT(id),
                    MPFROMP(pszText)))
This function requires the existence of a message queue.
