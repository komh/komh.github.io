WinSetClipbrdOwner - Remarks

The clipboard owner window receives the following clipboard-related messages at appropriate times:

    WM_DESTROYCLIPBOARD
    WM_HSCROLLCLIPBOARD
    WM_PAINTCLIPBOARD
    WM_RENDERALLFMTS
    WM_RENDERFMT
    WM_SIZECLIPBOARD
    WM_VSCROLLCLIPBOARD.
