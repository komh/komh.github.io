WinSetCp - Errors

Possible returns from WinGetLastError

    PMERR_INVALID_HMQ (0x1002)

      An invalid message-queue handle was specified.
    PMERR_RESOURCE_NOT_FOUND (0x100A)
      The specified resource identity could not be found.
