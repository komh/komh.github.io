WinPopupMenu Parameter - idItem

    idItem (LONG) - input

      Item identity.

      This is used if either the PU_POSITIONONITEM or PU_SELECTITEM of the fs parameter is also set. It must be greater or equal to 0 and less or equal to 0xFFFF.
