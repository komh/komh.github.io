WinIsMenuItemValid - Remarks

This macro expands to: 

#define WinIsMenuItemValid(hwndMenu, usId)
  ((BOOL)WinSendMsg(hwndMenu,
                    MM_ISITEMVALID,
                    MPFROM2SHORT(usId, TRUE),
                    MPFROMSHORT(FALSE)))

This function requires the existence of a message queue.
