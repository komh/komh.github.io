WinTrackRect Parameter - hwnd

    hwnd (HWND) - input

      Window handle where tracking is to take place.

      It is assumed that the style of this window is not WS_CLIPCHILDREN.

      HWND_DESKTOP

        Track over the entire screen
      Other
        Track over specified window only.
