WinQueryLboxSelectedItem - Remarks

This macro is defined as: 

#define WinQueryLBoxSelectedItem (hwndLbox)
        ((LONG)WinSendMsg(hwndLbox,
                          LM_QUERYSELECTION,
                          MPFROMLONG(LIT_FIRST),
                          (MPARAM)NULL))

This macro requires the existence of a message queue.
