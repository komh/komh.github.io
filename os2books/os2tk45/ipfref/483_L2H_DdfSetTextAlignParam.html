DdfSetTextAlign Parameter - fAlign

    fAlign (ULONG) - input

      Justification flag.

      Only the following constants may be used:

      TA_LEFT

        Left-justify text.
      TA_RIGHT
        Right-justify text.
      TA_CENTER
        Center text.
