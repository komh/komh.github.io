Notes - WinSetLangViewer

The "Language Viewer" window displays control and status information that is useful for users of a specific language.

The "Language Viewer" window is sent WM_LANG_INFO_CHANGED notification message by the system to inform it of significant events that are relevent to users of a specific language.
