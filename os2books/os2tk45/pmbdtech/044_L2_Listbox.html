Listbox

The &rtl behavior of the List Box results in the justification of the text in each listbox item. In a &rtl Listbox, the text of the items is right-justified (as opposed to left-justification in the original, &ltr Listbox).

In addition, the display of the text is done in the correct order, according to the bidi attributes of the listbox window.
