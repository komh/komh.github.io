WORDLENGTH

 컴훇ORDLENGTH(string,n)컴컴컴컴

WORDLENGTH returns the length of the n th blank-delimited word in string. n must be a positive whole number. If there are fewer than n words in the string, 0 is returned.

Here are some examples: 

WORDLENGTH('Now is the time',2)       ->    2
WORDLENGTH('Now comes the time',2)    ->    5
WORDLENGTH('Now is the time',6)       ->    0
