Literature

This section contains a list of publications (books, online documentations) that might be useful for REXX programming under OS/2.

The information in this section is collected from various sources, for example: magazines, mailbox- and CompuServe messages, catalogs and some other.

I've checked all information against one or more sources where possible but of course I can't make any warranties about the accuracy of the information.

If you're looking for additional literature about REXX it's a good choice to take a look at the literature provided by IBM.
