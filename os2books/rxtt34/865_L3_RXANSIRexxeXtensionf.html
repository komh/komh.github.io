Description from the author:

"RXANSI is a REXX extension for OS/2 1.2 EE and OS/2 1.3 SE. It was developed due to the lack of power available in REXX to perform screen I/O."

A highlight of this DLL is the mouse support for REXX textmode programs.

The DLL provides the following functions:

  • RxAnsiInit()
  • RxGoTo(x,y)
  • RxGetChar()
  • RxGetChars()
  • RxPutChars(x)
  • RxLine(x1,y1,x2,y2,n)
  • RxBox(x1,y1,x2,y2,Fill)
  • RxHMenu(x1,y1,options)
  • RxVMenu(x1,y1,options)
  • RxMode(mode,handle)
  • RxLineType(V,H)
  • RxMouse( < ON | OFF > )
