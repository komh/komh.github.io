RxComm provides serial(COM) port functions for REXX.

This DLL exports the following functions (as of v1.9):

  • RxCommLoadFuncs

  • RxCommDropFuncs

  • RxCommGetVersion

  • RxCommPort

  • RxCommOpen

  • RxCommClose

  • RxCommRead

  • RxCommWrite

  • RxCommSignal

  • RxCommBreak

  • RxCommSetup

  • RxCommOption

  • RxCommLog
