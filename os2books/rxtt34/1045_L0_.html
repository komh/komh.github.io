The lists of APARs (= Authorized Problem Analysis Reports) are available on the Web Site of IBM.. The filenames for the OS/2 WARP 3 APAR lists are 30APRn.* , the filenames for the OS/2 WARP 4 APAR lists are 40APRn.* (where n is a number). There is also an APAR search engine on the internet (see Internet - Web Pages)

The APARs referenced in this version are OS/2 WARP 3 and OS/2 WARP 4 APARs!
