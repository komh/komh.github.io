Name: RXDATE.*

RxDate is a REXX CMD containing some useful functions for working with dates:

  • Weekday
  • MonthName
  • Dow (= DayOfWeek)
  • IsLeap
  • DayOfYear
  • DaysInYear
  • DateDiff
