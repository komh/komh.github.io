Building Back Up History of the INI Files from STARTUP.CMD

REM *** Build Backup History ***
C:
CD \OS2\INSTALL
COPY OS2.3 OS2.OLD
COPY OS2.2 OS2.3
COPY OS2.1 OS2.2
COPY OS2.INI OS2.1
COPY OS2SYS.3 OS2SYS.OLD
COPY OS2SYS.2 OS2SYS.3
COPY OS2SYS.1 OS2SYS.2
COPY OS2SYS.INI OS2SYS.1
CD\
REM *** That's all folks ... ***
