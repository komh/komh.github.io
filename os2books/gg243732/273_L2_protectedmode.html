protected mode

Mode of operation for the Intel 80286 and 80386/80486 processors, whereby the address space is expanded to 16MB (80286) or 4GB (80386/80486), and memory references are translated via segment selector and offset, enabling full memory protection between processes executing in the system. With the 80386 and 80486, paging is available in protected mode.
